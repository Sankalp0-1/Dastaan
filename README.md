# Dastaan — Cultural Storytelling Chatbot 🌍

> Every culture has a story. Ask for yours.

---

## ✨ Overview

Dastaan is a purpose-built cultural storytelling chatbot designed to feel like a **guided exploration experience**, not a generic chat interface.

Instead of giving factual, list-based answers, Dastaan responds with **immersive, narrative-driven stories** that reflect how a local might describe their culture — rich in sensory details, emotions, and context.

---

## 🧠 Why I Built This

Most AI chatbots provide **dry, encyclopedic responses**.

I wanted to create something that feels:

- Human  
- Warm  
- Experiential  

Dastaan simulates the feeling of **learning culture through stories**, not facts — like listening to a traveler or local narrator.

---

## 🎯 Key Product Decisions (Frontend Thinking)

- **Guided Prompts**  
  Predefined prompts reduce friction and help users start exploring quickly.

- **Structured Story Responses**  
  Responses are divided into sections (e.g., atmosphere, traditions, invitation) for readability and engagement.

- **Follow-up Suggestions**  
  Each response includes contextual follow-ups to encourage deeper exploration.

- **Explore Page (Map + Grid)**  
  Users can visually discover cultures via a world map or directly select countries.

- **Visited Tracking**  
  Tracks explored cultures using localStorage to create a sense of progress and personalization.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🌍 **AI-powered storytelling**  
  Rich, narrative responses with sensory detail  

- ✨ **Follow-up suggestions**  
  Keeps the conversation flowing naturally  

- 🗺️ **World Map Explorer**  
  Interactive map with 40 cultures to explore  

- ✅ **Visited tracking**  
  Track explored cultures (persisted locally)  

- 🔍 **Search functionality**  
  Quickly find any country  

- 🌗 **Dark mode**  
  Consistent theming across the app  

- 📖 **Story of the Day**  
  A rotating prompt to inspire exploration  

---

## 🤖 AI & Prompt Design

The AI is guided using structured prompts to ensure:

- Story-like tone (not factual dump)  
- Sectioned responses  
- Cultural depth and sensory detail  
- Relevant follow-up questions
